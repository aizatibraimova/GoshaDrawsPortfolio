GULZADA OROZAKUNOVA

Hello and welcome! My name is Gulzada Orozakunova, and I am a custom portrait, illustration artist with a passion for digital art. I have been fascinated by digital art for a long time, and in 2019, I finally had the opportunity to dive into this world and monetize my hobby.
Since then, I have been using my skills and creativity to create beautiful, one-of-a-kind portraits and illustrations for my clients. I work primarily in the Procreate app, which I find to be an incredibly versatile and intuitive tool for digital art creation.
My portfolio is filled with a variety of works, each one unique and tailored to the specific needs and preferences of my clients. Whether you are looking for a special gift or want to commemorate a significant moment in your life, I am here to help you bring your vision to life.
I believe that art is an essential part of our lives, and I am passionate about creating works that are both beautiful and meaningful. I work closely with my clients to ensure that each piece captures the essence of the subject and reflects their unique personality and qualities.
If you are interested in seeing some of my works, please take a look at the samples I have posted here. You can also find more of my creations on my Social Media accounts, where I frequently share my latest artwork.
